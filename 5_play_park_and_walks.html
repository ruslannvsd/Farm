Play Park

Adventure Playground

Let your kids' imaginations soar as they explore our adventure playground, complete with climbing frames, slides, and interactive elements.

Petting Farm

Introduce your children to farm animals in our petting farm area, where they can interact with gentle and friendly animals under supervision.

Tractor Rides

Take a tractor ride around the farm with the whole family. Enjoy the scenic views and learn about the farm's history and operations.

Nature Trail

Embark on an adventure along our nature trail, where children can explore the beauty of nature and learn about plants and wildlife.

Our Play Park is designed to provide a safe and enjoyable experience for families. We have implemented safety measures such as soft ground surfaces, secure fencing, and supervision by trained staff to ensure a secure environment for children.

During their visit to the Play Park, children will also have the opportunity to participate in educational sessions on how to interact with animals safely and respectfully. These sessions are conducted by our knowledgeable staff who will teach children about proper animal handling techniques, the importance of gentle touch, and the significance of animal welfare.

Bring your little ones to our Play Park and watch them have a fantastic time in a fun-filled and family-friendly setting!

Walks

Woodland Trail

Enjoy a peaceful walk through our enchanting woodland trail. Experience the tranquility of nature and discover a variety of native trees and wildlife. Length: 2 miles, Difficulty: Easy.

River Path

Follow the meandering river path and soak in the picturesque views. Spot ducks, swans, and other water-loving creatures along the way. Length: 3.5 miles, Difficulty: Moderate.

Hilltop Vista

Climb up to our hilltop vista and be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Length: 1.5 miles, Difficulty: Moderate to Difficult.

Meadow Stroll

Take a leisurely stroll through our vibrant meadows, adorned with wildflowers. Enjoy the fresh air and open space. Length: 1 mile, Difficulty: Easy.

Explore the natural beauty of our farm by embarking on our scenic walks. Each walk offers unique points of interest and beautiful views, allowing visitors to connect with nature and enjoy the serene surroundings.

Whether you prefer an easy walk through the woodlands or a more challenging hike to the hilltop vista, our walks cater to different preferences and fitness levels. So put on your walking shoes and immerse yourself in the natural wonders of our farm!
