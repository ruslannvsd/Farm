Play Park & Walks

Adventure Playground

Let your kids' imaginations soar as they explore our adventure playground, complete with climbing frames, slides, and interactive elements.

Petting Farm

Introduce your children to farm animals in our petting farm area, where they can interact with gentle and friendly animals under supervision.

Tractor Rides

Take a tractor ride around the farm (one-two persons). Enjoy the scenic views and learn about the farm's history and operations.

Hiitop Vista

Climb up to our hilltop vista and be rewarded with breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding countryside. Length: 0.5 miles, Difficulty: Moderate to Difficult.

Visit our Play Park for a safe and enjoyable experience. Children can join educational sessions on interacting with animals responsibly, led by our knowledgeable staff who teach proper handling techniques, the importance of gentle touch, and animal welfare. To ensure a spot for your visit and make any necessary reservations, please contact us in advance.

Immerse yourself in the natural beauty of our farm through scenic walks. Each walk offers unique points of interest, allowing you to connect with nature and enjoy serene surroundings. Choose from easy woodland strolls to challenging hikes with panoramic hilltop vistas. Explore the wonders of our farm at your own pace.

Bring your little ones to our Play Park for a fantastic time in a fun-filled and family-friendly setting!
